About Us

Welcome to El Vado Mexican Restaurant

40 years have passed since the opening of our first restaurant on April 4, 1984 in Yecuatla, Veracruz.

"Sazón and Tradición" is what defines us, offering the best of Mexican food, their traditions and their flavors.

McHenry

In 2023 we landed in McHenry and we can only feel extremely grateful for the welcome they have given us, we have become one of their favorite restaurants. Therefore, every day we do our best to offer you the best of Mexican food, like the promos that you already know: Burrito Monday, Taco Tuesday, Imported Beer Wednesday, Margarita Thursday and Fajitas Friday. As well as an extensive assortment of dishes for every day.

Hello Lake Zurich

We are so excited to introduce El Vado #2 Mexican Restaurant. One little piece of our Mexico Lindo y querido, for everyone who wants to know the flavor of Veracruz. We will always be happy to serve you our dishes, 100% Mexican style. We will be open every day, so you can enjoy delicious tacos, meats, a warm soup and our fresh seafood. Also don't forget to try the drinks at the bar, Margaritas, Palomas, Cantaritos and Micheladas are our specialty!
